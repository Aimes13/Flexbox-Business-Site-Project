About Pello Photography

Photography is an art of capturing brief, beautiful moments and making them last for an eternity.

From a young age, this has been my aspiration:
  • To freeze a person's essence
  • To display marvellous perspective
  • To exhibit the simplicities of nature

Meet the Photographer

Anne-Marie Makombe, the sole photographer and owner of Pello Photograpy, has a diploma in Professional Photography. She specialises in macro photography, events photography and portraiture.

The business was started as a result of chasing a passion for photography and turning it into a career; it offers good quality images and affordable service. Shots are taken on location.
